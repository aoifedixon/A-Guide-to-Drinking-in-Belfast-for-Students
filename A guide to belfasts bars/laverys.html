About Us

Food

Food is available in all of the bars, including The Woodworkers, from noon to 10pm (12.30pm-10pm on Sunday), featuring traditional Irish cuisine, light bites and a fine array of contemporary fare, from juicy steak meal deals and Scooby Snack burgers to tasty Halloumi salads.

Pool Room

On the top floor is Northern Ireland’s largest pool room. There are 22 high quality and well-maintained pool tables, offering a premium experience for both club and casual players. Pool tables can be hired for £1 per game or £2.50 for three games, and they are also available for group bookings and corporate entertaining.

Sport

Keep up to date with all of the biggest sporting events including Rugby, Formula One, Football and Golf.
